About Our School

Established in 1904, Pt. Ls. Jha Govt. Model Hr. Sec. School of Excellence stands as a beacon of knowledge and academic brilliance in Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh. Founded by the visionary leader, Shri Ray Bahadur Pandit Lajja Shankar Jha, the school has been nurturing young minds with unwavering dedication for over a century. Nestled in the heart of South Civil Lines, our institution is more than just a school—it is a legacy of Excellence, Discipline, and Holistic development. With a rich history and a progressive approach, we continue to inspire, educate, and empower generations of students to become leaders of tomorrow.x
